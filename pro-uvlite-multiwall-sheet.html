ProUvlite Multiwall Sheet

Polycarbonate facades can have a great visual impact and not just on the outside. The material allows light to enter the building and distributes it evenly, creating luminous and bright spaces. This effect is enhanced when coloured Multiwall sheets are used, as the hue has a direct impact on the environment and creates great atmospheres. Hollow chamber are extremely versatile and differ from Thickness to thickness ProUvlite Multiwall Sheet are available in various thicknesses and can be produced in Standard widths of 1220mm and 2100mm and with closed edges according to customer requirements. Areas of application for the hollow chamber are primarily patio roofs, conservatories, pergolas and greenhouses - however, here too, it is true that almost any project can be realized from a purely structural point of view. Our portfolio for this product group is very diverse and ranges from 4 mm thick to a maximum of 40 mm thick. All products are available in different colours and variants. Of course, our double-wall panels are always produced with a co-extruded protective layer, so we can guarantee that they are protected against premature aging and yellowing for at least 10 years.
Advantages:

  • Polycarbonate is a high-impact thermoplastic
  • Temperature resistance from -40 to +115°C, briefly up to 130°C
  • Impact strength almost constant over this temperature range
  • Good long-term behaviour due to UV protection
